WHO I AM?

An aspiring spacecraft engineer focused on propulsion systems and thermal control, with unwavering devotion to astrophysics, particularly in the exploration of stellar phenomena.

Through my studies and personal curiosity, I explore how engineering and astrophysics intersect. This website is a space for me to share my research, articles, and insights on these topics as I continue to deepen my understanding of the universe.

I'm also interested in how emerging technologies and artificial intelligence are shaping the future of aerospace, which influences both my academic work and broader career perspective.

Feel free to reach out with any feedback on my writing. For more details on my academic and technical background, please refer to my Resume.
